ProAct Services Corporation Specializes in Providing Innovative Industrial Water Treatment and Industrial Air Treatment Services.

With an emphasis in on-site industrial water treatment, industrial air treatment, industrial degassing/vapor control, environmental remediation, and suspended solids removal, ProAct Services Corporation is committed to delivering exceptional service in a safe, responsive manner to ensure client satisfaction.

Our team of dedicated specialists, including environmental treatment specialists, project managers, field personnel, treatment system operators, skilled welders, mechanics, and electricians take pride in utilizing their unparalleled knowledge to design and build custom systems to operate at maximum efficiency. Headquartered in Michigan, with regional locations throughout the United States, ProAct Services Corporation is prepared to respond and mobilize to address immediate and emergent situations.
