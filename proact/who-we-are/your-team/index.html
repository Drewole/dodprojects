Your Team

Officers

  • Frank Smiddy

    Vice Chairman Director of Corporate Development

    Frank Smiddy has been designing, building and operating environmental remediation systems for over 30 years. After receiving his BS in Microbiology and minor in Chemistry from University of Massachusetts and MBA training at Notre Dame, Frank began his environmental career designing and installing activated carbon remediation systems for the cleanup of Underground Storage Tank sites.

    In 1994, Frank started ProAct Services Corporation. ProAct specializes in design, construction and operation of environmental remediation systems. Since its beginning, ProAct has completed over 3000 projects in 42 states, Canada, Mexico and Colombia, S.A.

  • Gerard Smiddy

    CEO/President

    Gerard Smiddy has worked as ProAct’s COO for over ten years and is responsible for the construction, installation and maintenance of environmental remediation equipment. His past experience includes project management and superintendent duties in commercial construction. Mr. Smiddy also attended The Westbrook College of Business and served in the military.

  • Dave Morrow

    Chief Operations Officer

    Dave Morrow has an extensive background in operational management and business systems knowledge. Working in both the Aerospace and Automotive industries over the past 40 years, Dave has been a key part of several large scale domestic and international business initiatives with a focus on value based systems development to support both organic and acquisition driven growth. Dave has a Business degree from Baker College to complement numerous certifications including Six Sigma, Lean Manufacturing, Operational Excellence, Enterprise Resource Planning and APICS Supply Chain Management.

  • Zach Nichols

    Chief Financial Officer

    Zach Nichols has greater than eight years of progressive accounting, financial reporting and management experience with companies ranging from $20 million to $4 billion in revenue. Prior to joining ProAct in 2017, Mr. Nichols was the Chief Financial Officer of the Harding Group; a heavy civil construction company headquartered in Indianapolis Indiana. Before joining Harding, Mr. Nichols was the CFO and COO of AI Innovations; a manufacturing firm headquartered in Fishers Indiana. In addition, Mr. Nichols started his career in public accounting for PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP. In this capacity, Mr. Nichols serviced public and private manufacturing/industrial product clients, where his expertise lies in business processes, internal controls, and financial reporting. Mr. Nichols is a Certified Public Accountant (CPA) and received his Master’s degree from the Kelley School of Business at Indiana University – Bloomington.
