Minnetonka Minnesota Community Profile

Staincrete is proud to offer its services to the good people of Minnetonka Minnesota. We thought you might like to know a bit more about this interesting and vibrant community.

History of Minnetonka Minnesota

The origin of the city’s name came from the Dakota word for water or “Minne” and “tonka” which means great. The city has a long and storied history going back to the 1850s where the 1st settlement began. At the time, the area was known as sacred ground for Native Americans. Most of the settlers came from New England, other parts of Minnesota and from the Czech Republic. Incorporation to cityhood was achieved in 1968.

Fun Things to Do in Minnetonka MN

The city maintains 5 community parks and 44 neighborhood parks. There are lots of places to enjoy your favorite sporting activities.

Minnetonka Civic Center – check out some music, films and theater performances in a picturesque setup. All events are free to the public.

Minnehaha Creek – this is a 22 mile long tributary that passes through Minnetonka, Hopkins and Saint Louis Park. Perfect for boating and fishing activities.

Bryant Lake Regional Park – features an 18-hole disc golf course, recreational areas with beach and boating amenities.
