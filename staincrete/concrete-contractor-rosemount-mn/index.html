History of Rosemount MN

The original settlers in Rosemount were the Lower Band Mdewakanton of the Santee Sioux tribe. However, Chief Little Crow on August 5, 1851, leader of the New Ulm Indian uprising signed a treaty that turned over the land to the federal government. The first white settlers came in 1853, William and Walter Srathern and CH Carr, originally from Scotland by way of NY to stake a claim. Soon after a lot of Irish immigrants, mostly Irish Catholics also settled. On May 11, 1858, settlers decided to organize for the first township meeting. The main topic that they discussed was the name of the town. Years before in 1855, the first post office was named Rosemount by Andrew Keegan and since there was a strong Irish influence and a village of the same name exists in Ireland., Rosemount was retained.

Restaurants & Nightlife in Rosemount MN

Rack Shack BBQ — Located at a strip mall and you order at the counter and they bring the food to your table. The pulled pork , corn bread and beans was absolutely fabulous. Do also try the Sammie beef brisket, mac and cheese, bacon tator salad and the beef brisket burger. Lots of sauces to choose from.

Rudy’s Redeye Grill — The prime rib is a must try and never disappoints. Food is always well prepared and seasoned very well. There is a nice patio for outdoor dining too. Do order their famous green beans and stuffed mushroom caps.

House of Coates — Great place to enjoy premium burgers. Do try the Swanee burger: half pound burger, bacon, blue grilled onions and cheddar. The Reuben with sweet potato fries is also fantastic and is perfect with pickle on the side. Try the Friday night fish fry which is absolutely fantastic.
